## Overview

JobHunt is a backend-driven job intelligence tool built to ingest, normalize, score, and expose job lead data through a usable product interface.

## What I built

- ingestion flows for job leads from multiple sources
- normalization and deduplication logic
- backend APIs consumed by a desktop client
- configurable scoring and filtering workflows
- a structure designed for repeatable scheduled runs
